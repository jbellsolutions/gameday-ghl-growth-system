## PHASE 1: BUILD IT INTERNALLY FIRST (Weeks 1-2)

### Week 1: GHL Workflows Build

**Goal:** Build the complete Go High Level automation system in our own GHL account so we can demo it, test it, and hand it off.

**Step 1 — Pipeline Architecture (Day 1)**
Build the 10-stage pipeline in GHL:

```
New Lead → Contacted → Consultation Booked → Consultation Completed →
Treatment Plan Presented → Membership Started → Active Patient →
At-Risk → Lapsed → Reactivated
```

Each stage gets its own automation triggers. This is the backbone everything else connects to.

**Step 2 — Speed-to-Lead Sequence (Day 1-2)**
Build the missed-call text-back + multi-step fast response:
- Immediate: Auto-text acknowledging inquiry
- +5 min: Second text with booking link
- +15 min: Voicemail drop or AI call
- +1 hour: Email with educational content (low T symptoms)
- +24 hours: SMS with patient testimonial + booking CTA
- +48 hours: Final "still interested?" text
- +7 days: Move to long-term nurture

**Step 3 — Reactivation Campaign (Day 2-3)**
Build the 90-day lapsed patient workflow:
- Day 1: SMS — "It's been a while since your last visit..."
- Day 2: Email — Educational content on what happens when TRT stops
- Day 5: SMS — "We have openings this week. Book your follow-up: [link]"
- Day 10: Task assigned to clinic staff for personal phone call
- Day 14: SMS with limited-time re-enrollment incentive
- Day 30: Final "we miss you" message

**Trigger:** If last appointment > 90 days AND status is not "Active" → move to Reactivation pipeline.

**Step 4 — No-Show Recovery (Day 3)**
- +15 min after missed appointment: SMS reschedule prompt
- +2 hours: Follow-up with one-click reschedule link
- +24 hours: Email on importance of consistent treatment
- +48 hours: Phone call task to staff

**Step 5 — Appointment Confirmation & Follow-Up (Day 3-4)**
Pre-appointment:
- Auto-confirmation SMS + email on booking
- 48-hour reminder with intake form link
- 24-hour reminder
- 1-hour reminder

Post-appointment:
- Same-day thank you SMS
- +24 hours: "How are you feeling?" check-in
- +3 days: Review request trigger
- +7 days: Educational content related to their treatment
- +30 days: Next appointment reminder

**Step 6 — Review Generation (Day 4)**
- After treatment completed → thank-you SMS
- +3 days: SMS with direct Google review link
- If 4-5 star review → auto-send video testimonial request
- If no review after 7 days → one gentle email reminder
- Never ask more than twice per quarter

**Step 7 — Referral Program (Day 4-5)**
- After 60 days as active patient → auto-send referral invitation
- Unique referral links/codes tracked in GHL
- Auto-reward both referrer and referee on first visit
- Quarterly "Refer a Friend" campaign blast

**Step 8 — Newsletter/Content Drip (Day 5)**
- Weekly SMS health tip (rotate: TRT, ED, weight loss, lifestyle)
- Bi-weekly educational email newsletter
- Monthly "Protocol Check" email with booking CTA
- Quarterly "State of Men's Health" authority piece

### Week 1 Deliverable:
A fully built, tested GHL sub-account with all 8 systems live. Every workflow documented. Every trigger tested with dummy data.

## ADDITIONAL BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES (Beyond the Core 3 Products)

These are additional services we could pitch Tony on top of the Game Day Game Plan. They come from researching what top-producing franchises and competitors are doing that Game Day isn't.

### Tier 1: Start Immediately (High Revenue, High Feasibility)

**1. Membership Tier Restructuring**
Game Day likely runs a flat membership. Designing tiered packages dramatically increases ARPU:

| Tier | Monthly Price | Includes |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Core | $199-249/mo | TRT (weekly injections), quarterly labs, provider consults |
| Performance | $349-449/mo | Core + ED treatment + peptides OR weight loss |
| Elite | $599-799/mo | Performance + IV therapy + advanced diagnostics + priority scheduling |
| Executive | $1,200-1,500/mo | Elite + concierge access + annual comprehensive panel + telehealth |

**2. GLP-1 Weight Loss Service Launch Package**
This is the single biggest gap. The GLP-1 market is $100B+, every competitor is adding semaglutide/tirzepatide, and Game Day franchisees need turnkey launch support (protocols, staff training, marketing collateral, pricing strategy, compliance docs). We could build and sell this as a launch package: $50-100K per engagement.

**3. Multi-Location Performance Dashboard**
With 400+ locations and multi-unit operators like Tony, the demand for standardized KPIs and benchmarking between locations is immediate. Revenue per patient, patient acquisition cost, retention rate, visit frequency, membership conversion — all in one view.

**4. Corporate Wellness Programs**
On-site testosterone screening events at companies, employer-sponsored memberships, executive health packages. This is a new patient acquisition channel, not a product:
- Corporate screening events: $5K-15K per event
- Employer-sponsored memberships: $100-300/employee/month
- Executive packages: $2K-5K per panel
- Recurring corporate contracts: $50K-200K/year per enterprise client

### Tier 2: Near-Term Strategic Plays

**5. Local Partnership Playbook** — Templated gym partnerships, sports league sponsorships (perfect "Game Day" brand fit), physician referral programs. Turnkey kits that any franchisee can deploy.

**6. Patient Lifecycle Automation** — Goes beyond GHL workflows into onboarding sequences, automated upsell triggers based on lifecycle stage, churn prediction, and proactive retention campaigns.

**7. Staff Training Platform** — LMS with certification tracking, solving the scaling problem of 400+ locations needing continuous training for 4-5 employees each.

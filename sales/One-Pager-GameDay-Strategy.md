# Game Day Franchises — Strategy & Game Plan

---

## The Two-Part Play

**Part 1: Fix the CRM (immediate revenue)** — Build the GHL automations that stop money from leaking out
**Part 2: Turn on the content engine (sustained growth)** — Content Multiplier builds the infrastructure, Genius Tap keeps it running

---

## Part 1: GHL Direct Response System

### Reactivation (Biggest Quick Win)

Lapsed patients are money sitting in the CRM doing nothing. A simple automated sequence brings them back.

**Example — 90-Day Lapsed Patient Workflow:**
> Day 1 SMS: "Hey Mike, it's been a while since your last visit at Game Day. Your testosterone levels may have shifted — time for a check-in?"
> Day 2 Email: Educational piece on what happens when TRT stops
> Day 5 SMS: "We have openings this week. Book your follow-up here: [link]"
> Day 10: Phone call task assigned to clinic staff
> Day 14 SMS: "Your complimentary recheck expires Friday — grab your spot: [link]"

**The math:** 500 lapsed patients x 10% recovery x $200/mo membership x 12 months = **$120,000/year per location**

### Speed-to-Lead (Highest Conversion Impact)

> A new lead fills out a form at 2:47pm.
> By 2:48pm they get: "Thanks for reaching out to Game Day! Want to book your free testosterone check? Here's a link: [calendar]"
> By 2:52pm (no reply): "Most guys are surprised how quick it is — 15 minutes, in and out, results on the spot. Any questions?"
> By 3:47pm: Email with a patient success story + FAQ
> Next morning: "Still thinking about it? Here's what to expect at your first visit..."

First clinic to respond wins ~80% of the time. This automation alone can boost conversion 30-40%.

### No-Show Recovery

> +15 min after missed appointment: "We noticed you missed your appointment. Want to reschedule? [one-click link]"
> +24 hours: "Staying consistent with your protocol matters — here's why [brief education]. Let's get you rebooked: [link]"

### Review Generation

> +3 days after visit: "Hey Mike — glad you came in. If you have 30 seconds, a quick Google review helps other guys find the care they need: [direct Google link]"

Frame it around helping other men, not helping the business. Men respond to purpose-driven asks.

## Part 2: Content Engine

### Step 1 — Content Multiplier (One-Time Foundation Build)

Record one provider video (10-60 min) about their expertise, patient stories, treatment philosophy. The Content Multiplier turns it into **32+ assets**:

- **Lead Magnet**: "The Game Day Guide to Men's Hormone Health" (PDF for the free T-test funnel)
- **Email Course**: "5 Signs Your Testosterone is Low" (7-day drip sequence loaded into GHL)
- **Sales Page**: Content for the free testosterone check landing page
- **Newsletter Templates**: 10-12 editions ready to deploy bi-weekly
- **Social Calendar**: 30 days of posts + a "5-Day Testosterone Challenge"
- **Case Study Package**: Patient transformation stories (anonymized)

### Step 2 — Genius Tap (Ongoing Monthly Engine)

One 90-minute Genius Tap extraction call per provider. System matches their voice to copywriter DNA, then produces **52+ pieces/month**:

- 10 LinkedIn posts/week in their voice
- 3 articles/week
- 2 newsletters/week
- Social captions, email sequences, video scripts

**Example output from one extraction:**
> LinkedIn post (Todd Brown DNA — mechanism positioning):
> "Most guys think low energy is just 'getting older.' It's not. There's a specific hormonal mechanism that starts shifting around age 35 — and a 15-minute test can tell you exactly where you stand. I see it every day at the clinic..."

**Cost per location:** $2,000 setup + $750/mo (~$12.50/piece)

All content feeds directly into GHL: newsletters → email campaigns, social content → social planner, lead magnets → funnel pages.

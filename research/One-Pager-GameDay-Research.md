## The Business

Game Day Men's Health is a men's hormone and wellness franchise founded in 2018. They've grown to **257+ locations** with **1,000+ territories awarded**, expanding into Canada. Each clinic runs lean (4-5 staff, M-F) with a sports-themed "man cave" vibe — leather chairs, ESPN, complimentary drinks, and on-site labs that deliver testosterone results in 15 minutes.

**Services:** TRT (injections, pellets, oral), peptide therapy, semaglutide weight loss, ED treatment (GAINSWave, P-Shot), vitamin/IV therapy

**Economics:** $1.89M avg revenue per location | $627K adjusted earnings | $150-250/mo patient memberships

## The CRM Situation (Go High Level)

Tony's franchises use GHL but he's doing a lot manually and isn't happy with the setup. The research found 6 specific pain points:

- **HIPAA compliance** costs $297/mo extra and still has gaps (default settings conflict with HIPAA)
- **No men's health/TRT snapshot exists** — everything must be custom-built from scratch
- **Two-system problem** — GHL handles marketing but can't replace their EMR, so data syncs manually
- **Workflows break** — phone numbers get flagged, deliverability drops, and with 4-5 staff nobody maintains it
- **Multi-location reporting is clunky** — each location is a separate sub-account with no easy rollup
- **Inconsistent follow-up** — the #1 revenue leak; without automation, leads go cold and marketing spend is wasted

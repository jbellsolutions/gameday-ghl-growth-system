## WHAT TOP-PRODUCING CLINICS DO (Best Practices Research)

### 1. Patient Reactivation Campaigns
**The Money Left on the Table**

Typical reactivation response rates: **4-8% per campaign**
- 500-patient dormant list × $250 avg appointment value = **$5,000-$10,000 per campaign run**
- For TRT clinics with recurring monthly patients, lifetime value is much higher

**Proven Reactivation Sequence:**
| Day | Channel | Message |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Day 1 | Email | Personalized reactivation — highlight overdue lab work or check-in |
| Day 3 | SMS | Short, casual: "Hey [Name], it's been a while. Free T-level check — when works?" |
| Day 7 | Email | Educational content: new treatments available, testosterone health tips |
| Day 10 | Voicemail Drop | Personal feel from the clinic |
| Day 14 | Email + SMS | Social proof: patient testimonials, before/after stories |
| Day 21 | Final SMS | Urgency/scarcity: "Last reminder — your complimentary recheck expires Friday" |

**GHL Implementation:**
- Trigger: Contact inactivity > 30/60/90 days
- Pipeline stage: "Reactivation" with sub-stages (Attempted, Engaged, Booked, Won Back, Lost)
- Secondary campaign if no response after first sequence (different messaging angle)
- Tag-based segmentation: Last service type, last visit date, treatment protocol

### 2. Lead Nurture (New Leads → Booked Appointments)

**Speed-to-Lead is Everything:**
- Respond within 5 minutes of inquiry (GHL auto-response + SMS)
- AI-assisted conversation bot for after-hours
- Automated appointment booking link in every touchpoint

**Recommended Pipeline Stages (TRT/Men's Health Specific):**
1. **New Lead** — Form fill, phone call, or ad click
2. **Contacted** — First outreach made (within 5 minutes of lead entry)
3. **Consultation Booked** — Free testosterone test scheduled

6. **Membership Started** — Enrolled in monthly program

8. **At-Risk** — Missed appointment or late on protocol

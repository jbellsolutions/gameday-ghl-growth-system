# Dr. Leigh Vinocur — Research Overview

---

## Who She Is

**Leigh Vinocur, MD, MS, FACEP** — a board-certified emergency physician with 35+ years of clinical experience, a dual residency in urology and emergency medicine (extremely rare), a Johns Hopkins patient safety diplomate, and one of the most media-experienced physician voices in the country.

She graduated with high honors from University of Michigan, earned her MD from Wayne State, and later became one of the first people in the US to earn an MS in Medical Cannabis Science from the University of Maryland School of Pharmacy.

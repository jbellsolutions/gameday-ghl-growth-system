# Game Day Men's Health: Non-CRM Business Opportunities

## Research Date: March 29, 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Game Day Men's Health has scaled to 400+ locations with $1.8M average gross revenue per clinic and a lean 4-5 person staffing model. Their current franchise support covers 40 hours classroom + 24 hours on-the-job training, proprietary software, a franchisee intranet, and a dedicated business coach. Below are actionable opportunities beyond CRM and content that represent real revenue potential for us as a service provider.

---

## 1. FRANCHISE OPERATIONS SUPPORT

### What Franchisees Struggle With

- **Staff training beyond initial onboarding**: Game Day provides opening training but ongoing skill development, refresher training, and new-hire onboarding at scale (400+ locations) creates constant demand
- **SOP documentation and compliance**: HIPAA education, clinical competency assessments, patient experience protocols, complaint handling procedures
- **Weekly injection visit optimization**: Patients return weekly for 3-5 minute visits -- optimizing patient flow, wait times, and throughput directly impacts revenue per hour
- **Medical director oversight at scale**: Each clinic needs a physician medical director (urology, endocrinology, or anesthesiology background) plus clinic directors (NPs/PAs) for day-to-day operations

### Specific Services We Could Offer

1. **Digital SOP Library & Training Platform** -- Build and maintain a living SOP system with video walkthroughs, quizzes, and role-play simulations for each clinic role. Trainual-style platform customized for Game Day protocols.
2. **Patient Experience Audit Program** -- Mystery patient visits, Google review analysis, NPS benchmarking across locations, and actionable improvement plans.
3. **Staff Recruitment Pipeline** -- NP/PA sourcing is the bottleneck for opening new locations. A recruiting service or partnership with staffing agencies specifically for men's health clinicians.
4. **Compliance Monitoring** -- Automated HIPAA compliance checks, state-by-state regulatory tracking (critical as they expand across states with different TRT/compounding regulations).

## 2. REVENUE EXPANSION OPPORTUNITIES

### New Treatment Lines Game Day Should Add (That We Could Help Implement)

**HIGH PRIORITY -- Already Adjacent to Current Services:**

| Service | Market Size | Avg Revenue/Patient/Month | Implementation Complexity |
|---------|------------|---------------------------|--------------------------|
| GLP-1 Weight Loss (Semaglutide/Tirzepatide) | $100B+ market | $300-500/mo | Medium -- requires prescribing protocol updates |
| Peptide Therapy (BPC-157, CJC-1295, etc.) | $80B projected market | $200-400/mo | Medium -- FDA oversight tightening in 2026 |
| IV Therapy / NAD+ Infusions | Growing rapidly | $150-300/session | Low -- minimal equipment, high margin |
| Advanced Longevity Diagnostics | Emerging | $500-2,000/panel | Medium -- lab partnerships needed |

**MEDIUM PRIORITY -- Natural Extensions:**

- **Hair Restoration** (PRP, oral finasteride/minoxidil) -- fits the male demographic perfectly
- **Sexual Health Expanded** -- P-shot (PRP), acoustic wave therapy, Peyronie's disease treatment
- **Sleep Optimization Programs** -- Sleep studies referral partnerships + supplement protocols
- **Mental Health / Cognitive Performance** -- Nootropic protocols, stress management, counseling referrals

**Regulatory Warning**: FDA is tightening oversight on compounding practices and marketing claims for peptides in 2026. Payment processors are also restricting what they consider "high-risk" medical services including peptide therapies. Any implementation needs compliance-first approach.

### What We Could Build

1. **New Service Launch Playbook** -- For each treatment line: protocol development, staff training materials, marketing collateral, pricing strategy, compliance documentation
2. **Membership Tier Restructuring** -- Current model is likely a flat membership. Designing Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum tiers that bundle core TRT with add-on services increases ARPU significantly
3. **Revenue Optimization Consulting** -- Analyzing per-location data to identify which add-on services perform best in which markets

## 3. LOCAL MARKETING & COMMUNITY BUILDING

### Gym Partnerships Program

Game Day's "man cave" clinic environment (flat screens, leather chairs, snacks) already signals their brand identity. Gym partnerships are a natural fit:

- **Co-branded referral programs** with local gyms, CrossFit boxes, and fitness studios
- **Free testosterone screening events** hosted at partner gyms (Game Day already offers free 15-minute T tests)
- **Supplement and recovery partnerships** -- Joint promotions with sports nutrition retailers
- **Gym owner "ambassador" program** -- Gym owners who refer clients get ongoing referral fees

### Sports & Community Sponsorships

- **Local sports league sponsorships** -- Adult flag football, softball, basketball leagues (perfect brand alignment with "Game Day" name)
- **Youth sports sponsorships** -- Reaches fathers who are the target demo
- **First responder and veteran programs** -- Discounted memberships for police, fire, military (high-trust community marketing)
- **Men's health awareness events** -- Movember partnerships, prostate cancer screening drives, Father's Day health fairs

### Physician Referral Network

- **Primary care physician outreach** -- Many PCPs don't specialize in TRT and would happily refer patients to a trusted specialist
- **Urologist collaboration** -- Bi-directional referral relationship for complex cases
- **Corporate health screening partnerships** -- On-site testosterone screening at companies (see Section 4 below)

### What We Could Build

1. **Local Partnership Playbook** -- Templated gym partnership agreements, co-marketing materials, referral tracking systems
2. **Community Event Kit** -- Turnkey event planning packages (screening events, sponsorship activations, health fair booths)
3. **Physician Referral Program** -- CRM-integrated referral tracking, automated thank-you communications, referral volume reporting by physician
4. **Reputation Management** -- Systematic Google review generation (they have 50,000+ already -- scaling this across 400+ locations)

## 4. CORPORATE WELLNESS & EMPLOYER PARTNERSHIPS

### The Opportunity

56% of Americans are interested in subscription health plans (Tebra 2025 survey). Corporate wellness is a massive untapped channel for Game Day:

### Specific Programs We Could Build

1. **Employer Testosterone Screening Program** -- On-site or voucher-based free T-level checks for employees at partner companies. Targets: construction, law enforcement, fire departments, tech companies, financial services
2. **Corporate Membership Packages** -- Discounted group rates for employer-sponsored men's health memberships. Employer pays base, employee pays upgrade tiers
3. **Executive Health Packages** -- Premium comprehensive panels (hormones, metabolic, cardiac markers, longevity biomarkers) marketed to C-suite and senior leadership
4. **Workers' Comp / Occupational Health Adjacent** -- Partnering with occupational health providers for post-injury recovery optimization
5. **Insurance / HSA / FSA Integration** -- Helping clinics structure services to be HSA/FSA eligible where possible

### Revenue Model

- Corporate screening events: $5,000-15,000 per event
- Employer-sponsored memberships: $100-300/employee/month (employer subsidized)
- Executive packages: $2,000-5,000 per panel
- Recurring corporate contracts: $50,000-200,000/year per enterprise client

## 5. TECHNOLOGY & AUTOMATION (Beyond GHL)

### AI and Automation Opportunities

| Technology | Problem It Solves | Impact |
|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| AI appointment scheduling | No-show reduction, optimal scheduling | 80%+ reduction in wait times per industry data |
| AI clinical documentation | Provider charting burden | 72% reduction in charting time, 2 hrs saved/provider/day |
| Automated lab result delivery | Patient waiting for results, staff phone time | Instant delivery with AI-generated plain-language summaries |
| Predictive no-show analytics | Revenue loss from missed appointments | 15-25% no-show reduction |
| AI-powered patient follow-up | Lapsed patient reactivation | Automated outreach to patients who miss refill windows |

### Patient Portal & Telehealth

- **Custom patient portal** -- Lab results, appointment booking, medication refill requests, treatment history, educational content
- **Telehealth add-on** -- Follow-up consultations via video (not every visit needs to be in-clinic, especially for stable patients)
- **Remote monitoring integration** -- Wearable data integration (sleep, HRV, activity) to personalize treatment protocols
- **AI chatbot for patient FAQs** -- Handles the 80% of questions that are repetitive (side effects, injection technique, appointment scheduling)

### What We Could Build

1. **Integrated Patient Experience Platform** -- Combines portal, telehealth, lab results, and AI follow-up into one system
2. **AI Documentation Assistant** -- Trained on Game Day's specific protocols for faster, more accurate clinical notes
3. **Smart Scheduling System** -- AI-optimized scheduling that accounts for visit type, provider availability, and patient preferences
4. **Automated Patient Lifecycle Engine** -- Triggers based on patient milestones (90-day check-in, annual labs due, birthday outreach, lapsed visit alerts)

## 6. FRANCHISE EXPANSION SUPPORT (Multi-Unit Operators)

### What Multi-Location Owners Need

Game Day has franchisees operating multiple locations. These operators need tools that single-unit owners don't:

1. **Multi-Location Performance Dashboard**
   - Standardized 10-15 core KPIs across all locations
   - Location-level drill-down with franchisee self-service access
   - Real-time comparison and benchmarking between locations
   - Revenue per patient, patient acquisition cost, retention rate, average visit frequency, membership conversion rate

2. **Standardized Training at Scale**
   - LMS (Learning Management System) with certification tracking
   - New location launch checklist automation
   - Cross-location staff sharing/floating protocols

3. **Financial Benchmarking**
   - P&L comparisons across locations
   - Cost-per-acquisition benchmarking
   - Supply chain pricing optimization (bulk purchasing leverage)
   - Labor cost optimization modeling

4. **Expansion Planning Tools**
   - Territory analysis and demographic scoring
   - Site selection data (proximity to gyms, demographics, competition mapping)
   - New location launch timeline and milestone tracking

### What We Could Build

- **Franchise Intelligence Dashboard** -- Built on a BI platform, pulling data from GHL, POS, EMR, and financial systems into one view
- **Location Launch Automation** -- Checklist-driven project management for opening new locations (equipment procurement, staff hiring, marketing ramp, grand opening)
- **Benchmarking Reports** -- Monthly/quarterly performance reports with peer comparisons and actionable recommendations

## 7. PATIENT RETENTION & UPSELL OPTIMIZATION

### Current State

Game Day operates on a recurring membership model. Healthcare subscription models report 80%+ retention rates. Key optimization areas:

### Membership Tier Design

| Tier | Monthly Price | Includes | Target Patient |
|------|--------------|----------|----------------|
| **Core** | $199-249/mo | TRT (weekly injections), quarterly labs, provider consults | New patients, cost-conscious |
| **Performance** | $349-449/mo | Core + ED treatment + peptides OR weight loss | Patients wanting more optimization |
| **Elite** | $599-799/mo | Performance + IV therapy + advanced diagnostics + priority scheduling | High-income, optimization-focused |
| **Executive** | $1,200-1,500/mo | Elite + concierge access + annual comprehensive panel + telehealth unlimited | C-suite, athletes, biohackers |

### Patient Lifecycle Management

- **Onboarding (Days 1-30)**: Welcome sequence, treatment education, expectation setting, first follow-up scheduling
- **Optimization (Days 31-90)**: Lab result reviews, dosage adjustments, introduction to add-on services
- **Retention (Days 91-365)**: Regular check-ins, progress tracking, annual comprehensive review
- **Expansion (Year 2+)**: Upsell to higher tiers, referral program activation, longevity services introduction
- **Win-back (Lapsed)**: Automated outreach at 14, 30, 60, 90 days post-lapse with escalating offers

### What We Could Build

1. **Membership Optimization Engine** -- Data-driven tier recommendations based on patient profile, usage patterns, and health goals
2. **Automated Upsell Sequences** -- Triggered campaigns for add-on services based on patient lifecycle stage
3. **Referral Program** -- Structured patient referral program with incentives (account credits, free add-on sessions)
4. **Churn Prediction Model** -- AI model that identifies patients at risk of canceling based on visit frequency, engagement metrics, and satisfaction scores

## 8. COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE

### What Competitors Are Doing That Game Day Isn't (or Could Do Better)

**Hone Health (Online/Telehealth)**
- Tests 50+ health markers in initial blood panel (broader than standard T + E2)
- $149/month all-inclusive pricing
- Pure telehealth with home delivery -- no clinic visits required
- Strong content/education platform

**Marek Health (Health Optimization)**
- Holistic approach beyond hormones -- personalized health coaching, diagnostic interpretation, self-service testing
- Custom lab packages
- Positioned as "optimization" not just "treatment"
- Appeals to biohacker/longevity crowd

**TRT Nation (Budget)**
- $99/month -- lowest price in market
- Pure affordability play
- Minimal overhead, maximum accessibility

**Peter MD / Peter Uncaged (DTC)**
- Budget-friendly online TRT
- Heavy social media presence
- Targets younger demographic (30-40)

**Vault Health (Premium/In-Home)**
- At-home nurse practitioner visits
- Premium positioning
- In-home blood draws and exams
- NYC/Nashville/Dallas/Florida markets

### Key Trends Game Day Should Capitalize On

1. **GLP-1 Weight Loss** -- The biggest revenue opportunity in men's health right now. Every competitor is adding this. Game Day needs it yesterday.
2. **Longevity Medicine** -- NAD+ infusions, advanced biomarker panels, peptide protocols. The "optimization" market is growing faster than the "treatment" market.
3. **AI-Powered Personalization** -- Competitors are using AI for treatment protocol optimization, lab interpretation, and patient communication.
4. **Hybrid Telehealth + In-Clinic** -- Stable patients don't need weekly clinic visits. Offering telehealth for follow-ups while keeping in-clinic for injections and labs is the hybrid model winning in 2026.
5. **Men's Mental Health** -- Anxiety, depression, cognitive performance. The stigma is dropping fast and men are increasingly seeking help. This is a natural adjacency.
6. **Proactive/Preventive Positioning** -- Shifting from "fix your low T" to "optimize your health for the next 40 years" broadens the addressable market dramatically.

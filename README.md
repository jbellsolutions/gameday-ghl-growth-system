## What's Inside

This repo has seven systems that work together. You don't need to understand how they work — just what they do and what they'll produce for you.

### 1. GHL Workflows (Your CRM on Autopilot)

Right now you're doing a lot of this manually. These are the automations that replace that:

- **Speed-to-lead** — Every new inquiry gets a response in under 2 minutes (text + email + voicemail drop). The first clinic to respond wins ~80% of the time.
- **Database reactivation** — Patients who haven't been back in 90+ days get a 6-touch sequence pulling them back in. This alone is worth $96K-$216K/year per location.
- **No-show recovery** — Missed appointment? Auto-reschedule sequence fires within 15 minutes. Recovers 25-35% of no-shows.
- **Appointment reminders** — 48hr, 24hr, 1hr before. Plus post-visit follow-up and review request.
- **Review generation** — 3 days after a visit, patients get a direct link to leave a Google review. Compounds your local SEO over time.
- **Referral program** — After 60 days as an active patient, auto-invite to refer friends. Both get rewarded.
- **Newsletter drip** — Weekly health tips via SMS, bi-weekly email newsletter, monthly protocol check-in.

### 6. Power Partnerships (Jay Abraham's Methodology Applied to Your Clinics)

This maps out exactly who you should partner with and how:

| Partner Type | Who | Example |
|-------------|-----|---------|
| **BEFORE** | Businesses your patient uses before they need you | Gyms, personal trainers, sports nutrition |
| **DURING** | Businesses serving your patient at the same time | Chiropractors, nutritionists, sleep specialists |

The system finds specific businesses in your market, scores them, generates partnership proposals, and feeds them into the outbound engine.

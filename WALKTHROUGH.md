## Part 2: Installation (When You're Ready)

### Before You Start

You'll need:
- [ ] Your Go High Level account login
- [ ] Access to your GHL sub-account for each clinic location
- [ ] A computer with Claude Code installed
- [ ] About 2-3 hours for the full setup (or we can do it in stages)

### Step 1: GHL Workflows

**What happens:** We build 8 automation workflows in your GHL account.

**What you do:**
1. Log into your GHL account
2. Tell Claude Code: "Set up the GHL workflows for my Game Day clinic"
3. Claude will walk you through connecting your GHL account, building each pipeline stage, and configuring each workflow
4. We test each workflow with dummy data before going live

**The 8 workflows:**
- Speed-to-lead (auto-respond in under 2 minutes)
- 90-day database reactivation
- No-show recovery
- Appointment confirmation + follow-up
- Review generation
- Referral program
- Newsletter/content drip
- Long-term lead nurture

**Time:** ~45 minutes

### Step 2: Expert Series v3

**What happens:** We capture your expertise and turn it into marketing assets.

**What you do:**
1. Record a 30-90 minute video talking about your Game Day experience, what you see men struggling with, your philosophy, patient stories (anonymized)
2. Tell Claude Code: "Run the Expert Series on my video"
3. The system produces 32+ assets: lead magnets, email courses, newsletters, sales pages, social calendars, video scripts, case studies

**Time:** 90 minutes for the video + overnight for content generation

### Step 3: LinkedIn Autopilot

**What happens:** We set up autonomous LinkedIn authority building.

**What you do:**
1. Tell Claude Code: "Set up LinkedIn Autopilot for my Game Day clinic"
2. Configure your voice profile and content sources
3. Review 5 test posts — confirm they sound like you
4. Approve dry-run mode for the first week (you review every post before it publishes)
5. After first week, switch to full autopilot

**Time:** ~30 minutes setup + 1 week of review

### Step 4: Power Partnerships

**What happens:** We map your ideal partner ecosystem using Jay Abraham's methodology.

**What you do:**
1. Answer 12-15 questions about your business (the intake)
2. The system maps your Before/During/After/In-Conjunction partners
3. It researches specific businesses in your market and scores them
4. It generates partnership proposals and outreach sequences

**The questions cover:**
- What assets do you have that partners would value? (your labs, your patients, your brand)
- Who does your patient buy from BEFORE they need you? (gyms, trainers)
- Who serves them at the SAME TIME? (chiropractors, nutritionists)
- Who do they go to AFTER? (longevity clinics, executive health)
- What does a good partnership look like to you?

**Time:** ~20 minutes for intake + overnight for research and content

### Step 5: GTM Company (Outbound)

**What happens:** We configure outbound across three tracks.

**What you do:**
1. Tell Claude Code: "Set up GTM outbound for Game Day"
2. Choose your tracks:
   - Corporate wellness (HR directors, benefits managers)
   - Gym partnerships (local gyms, CrossFit, fitness studios)
   - Physician referrals (PCPs, urologists, sports medicine)
3. The system builds prospect lists, researches each one, and drafts personalized outreach
4. You review and approve every email before it sends (nothing goes out without you)

**Time:** ~30 minutes setup + ongoing review of drafts

### Step 6: AI Integrator Placement (Optional)

**What happens:** We place a trained person to run all of this daily.

**What you decide:**
- **Option A:** We place an integrator ($1K + $300/mo). They run everything. You manage them. We train and support them.
- **Option B:** You run it yourself or assign someone on your team. We do a one-time $1K setup and hand over docs + training.

**Time:** ~1 hour for onboarding call

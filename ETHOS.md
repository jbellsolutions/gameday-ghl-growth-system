# ETHOS — Game Day GHL Growth System

## Mission
Deliver measurable revenue growth to Game Day Men's Health franchises through automated CRM systems, trained AI integrators, and voice-matched content engines.

## Development Principles

1. **Revenue-first** — Every deliverable must connect to a measurable revenue outcome. Content without distribution strategy is waste. Automations without KPIs are decoration.

2. **Compliance-first for healthcare** — HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable. No patient data in this repo. No PII in templates. Every workflow must be reviewed against healthcare marketing regulations before deployment.

3. **Franchise-scalable** — Everything built for one location must work for 250+. No bespoke solutions that break at scale. Templates over custom builds. Documentation over tribal knowledge.

4. **Voice authenticity over volume** — The Expert Series exists to amplify the real voice of real providers. Never fabricate stories, credentials, or patient experiences. Every piece must pass the "would they actually say this?" test.

5. **Operator-empowering** — The goal is to make Tony's team more capable, not more dependent. Systems should transfer knowledge, not create vendor lock-in.

## Quality Bar
- GHL workflows must be tested with dummy data before client deployment
- Content pieces must pass the 3-agent quality gauntlet (Creator > Critic > Approver, score 75+)
- Revenue projections must state assumptions and show conservative/moderate/aggressive ranges
- All research must cite sources

## What This Repo Will Never Do
- Store patient data or protected health information
- Make medical claims or treatment recommendations
- Deploy untested automations to live patient databases
- Publish client voice profiles or proprietary story banks
- Guarantee specific revenue outcomes (projections are estimates, not promises)
